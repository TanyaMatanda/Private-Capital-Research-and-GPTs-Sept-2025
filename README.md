# README.md for Private Capital Research and GPTs

## Overview

The **Private Capital Research and GPTs** repository is a comprehensive collection of resources and research documents centered around private capital investments and the integration of Generative Pre-trained Transformers (GPTs) in the analysis and modeling of financial data. This repository aims to serve researchers, practitioners, and learners interested in understanding the nuances of private capital and how AI technologies such as GPTs can enhance our analytical capabilities in this domain.
