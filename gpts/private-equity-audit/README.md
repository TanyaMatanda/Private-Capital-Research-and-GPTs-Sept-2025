# Private Equity Audit & Risk Oversight GPT

Purpose
-------
This GPT assists investment committees, internal audit, and risk teams with rapid review and synthesis of private equity operational and risk documentation. It is designed to:
- Summarize key operational controls and gaps from fund documentation and memos.
- Generate audit checklists and suggested diligence questions.
- Produce risk-overview briefs for governance committees.

Intended users
--------------
- Audit and risk teams
- Investment committee members
- LP governance teams and compliance officers

Inputs
------
- Investment memos, fund operating agreements, risk and controls descriptions (paste text or attach excerpts).
- Targeted question or request (e.g., "Summarize operational risks", "Create a 1-page audit checklist").

Outputs
-------
- Short executive summary (3–6 bullet points)
- Risk checklist mapped to control owners
- Suggested follow-up due diligence questions
- Example remediation options and risk severity rating (Low/Medium/High)

Privacy & data handling
-----------------------
- Do not provide confidential attachments containing PII, privileged legal communications, or secrets.
- Prefer redacted excerpts for any documents you paste.

Limitations
-----------
- Not a substitute for a formal third-party audit or legal advice.
- Outputs depend on the completeness and clarity of the input text.

Using this artifact
-------------------
- Prompts and templates are in prompts/.
- Examples and test cases are in examples/.
- Add a metadata.json with versioning and ownership details.
